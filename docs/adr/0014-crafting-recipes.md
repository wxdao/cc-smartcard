# Crafting Recipes

Smart Cards are crafted from paper, redstone dust, and a copper ingot. Optional dyes may be added while crafting a new Smart Card to produce a dyed card, matching ComputerCraft's dyed disk recipe style. Existing Smart Cards may also be dyed or cleared through CC: Tweaked's dyeable item recipes without changing their identity, label, issued state, or storage.

This keeps cards cheap enough for common credential uses while using copper as the smart-card contact material. The reader recipe remains visibly related to the disk drive without being identical to it.
