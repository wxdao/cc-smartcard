# Crafting Recipes

Smart Cards are crafted from paper, redstone dust, and a copper ingot. Card Readers follow the ComputerCraft disk drive recipe shape, replacing the lower redstone dust with a copper ingot: stone across the top, stone-redstone-stone in the middle, and stone-copper-stone on the bottom.

This keeps cards cheap enough for common credential uses while using copper as the smart-card contact material. The reader recipe remains visibly related to the disk drive without being identical to it.
