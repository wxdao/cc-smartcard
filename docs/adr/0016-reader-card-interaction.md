# Reader Card Interaction

Players insert a smart card by right-clicking a Card Reader while holding a Smart Card, and remove the inserted card by right-clicking the reader with an empty hand. Right-clicking with other items does not remove the card, avoiding accidental removal while building or using tools.

The reader stores the inserted card as an item stack and renders a visible card on the block while occupied. Breaking the reader drops both the reader block and the inserted card as separate items.
